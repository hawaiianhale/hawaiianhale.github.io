The Kī Plant

Scientific Name:

Cordyline fruticosa

Common Name:

Ti Plant

Cultural Uses

Leaves: thatch, food wrappers, hula skirts, sandals. The sweet roots were baked and eaten or used to make an alcoholic beverage called ‘okolehao.
